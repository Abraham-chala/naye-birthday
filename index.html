Dear Naye,

My dearest Naye,

Before I ever heard your voice, someone told me about you — and somehow, just the idea of you was enough to make me search the whole of Facebook until I found you.

I sent you that message with all the confidence I could carry — "give me your number and you'll fall in love within a month." You said "we'll see" and sent it to me. But the truth is, I kept that number quietly for an entire year — too in awe of you to press call.

Then fate did what I couldn't. It brought you to Ginchi. And suddenly, the girl I'd been carrying in my heart was standing right in front of me.

A friend introduced us as strangers. You had no idea it was me. I knew exactly who you were. And from that day forward, something quiet and beautiful grew between us — something you've never said out loud, but I have felt in every single moment.

In the way you stay. In the way you show up. In the way you smile when you think no one is watching. Naye — I see all of it. And I love all of it.

You don't need to say the words. I already know. And I will keep knowing it, for as long as you let me.

Happy birthday to the girl who made a nervous boy brave enough to type "hey" — and lucky enough to have her become his entire world.

Yours, forever  ♡
